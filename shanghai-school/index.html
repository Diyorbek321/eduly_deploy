Ask Us Anything

What is the duration of the language courses?

Our standard courses typically last between 3 to 6 months, depending on the intensity and level. Intensive courses are also available for those who want to learn faster.

Do you provide international certificates?

Yes, all successful graduates receive a certificate that is recognized by our global partner institutions and can be used for employment and academic applications.

What IT skills are taught in the Pro course?

The Pro course covers modern web development stacks including React, Node.js, and advanced database management, along with real-world project experience.

Can I study two courses simultaneously?

Yes, it is possible to combine courses, though we recommend consulting with a mentor first to ensure the study load is manageable for you.
